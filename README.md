## 📂 Projects

### 🛒 [Grocery Inventory Dead Stock Analysis](https://github.com/minse0321/grocery-inventory-dead-stock-analysis)
Analyzed 989 grocery products to identify dead stock and quantify capital inefficiency by category.  
→ $74,918 in tied-up inventory identified · Oils & Fats had the highest dead stock rate at 27.3%

### 🎬 [Netflix Content Analysis](https://github.com/minse0321/netflix-content-analysis)
Analyzed 9,827 Netflix titles to uncover what drives content quality and platform strategy.  
→ K-content ranked #1 in avg IMDb rating · Documentary outperformed Drama despite lower volume

### 🎵 [Wavelength Music App — User Behavior Analysis](https://github.com/minse0321/wavelength-music-app-analysis)
Simulated 800 users × 60 days of streaming behavior to understand retention and subscription drivers.  
→ Early engagement (plays & skips in first 3 days) is the strongest predictor of premium conversion

### ⚾ [Shohei Ohtani Career Batting Analysis](https://github.com/minse0321/ohtani-analysis)
Analyzed MLB Statcast data (2018–2025) using pybaseball, SQLite, and Tableau.  
→ Exit velocity rose from 92.6 to 95.7 mph · Peak performance came after joining the Dodgers
