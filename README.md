## 📂 Projects

### 🛒 [Grocery Inventory Dead Stock Analysis](https://github.com/minse0321/grocery-inventory-dead-stock-analysis)
**Question:** Which product categories are accumulating dead stock, and how much capital is being locked up?  
**Approach:** Engineered a Dead Stock Score combining stock quantity and turnover rate to flag underperforming products, then quantified tied-up inventory value by category.  
**Insight:** $74,918 locked across 192 dead stock products · Oils & Fats had the worst dead stock rate (27.3%) · Fruits & Vegetables accounted for the largest capital loss ($26,059)

---

### 🎬 [Netflix Content Analysis](https://github.com/minse0321/netflix-content-analysis)
**Question:** As the OTT market grows, what types of content actually drive quality — and where should platforms invest?  
**Approach:** Analyzed 9,827 titles across genre, language, IMDb score, and popularity using Python and Tableau.  
**Insight:** Volume does not equal quality — Drama dominates in count but falls below average rating · K-content ranked #1 in avg IMDb score across all languages · Documentary outperformed all high-volume genres in viewer satisfaction

---

### 🎵 [Wavelength Music App — User Behavior Analysis](https://github.com/minse0321/wavelength-music-app-analysis)
**Question:** What early user behaviors predict whether a streaming app user will convert to a paid subscription?  
**Approach:** Simulated 800 users × 60 days of event data, then analyzed retention curves, funnel drop-off, and persona segments.  
**Insight:** Users who play more and skip less in the first 3 days are significantly more likely to convert · Biggest funnel drop-off occurs at play_song → subscribe_premium (28% conversion)

---

### ⚾ [Shohei Ohtani Career Batting Analysis](https://github.com/minse0321/ohtani-analysis)
**Question:** How has Ohtani's batting performance actually changed across his career — and can we prove it with data, not intuition?  
**Approach:** Collected MLB Statcast data (2018–2025) via pybaseball API, stored in SQLite, queried with Window Functions and LAG(), visualized in Tableau.  
**Insight:** Exit velocity rose from 92.6 to 95.7 mph over career · Peak performance came after joining the Dodgers · 2021 marked the biggest single-season HR jump (+39)
